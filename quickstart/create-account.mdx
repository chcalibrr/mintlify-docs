#### Begin by accepting the email invite from Chatform and create your account:

To create your account on Chatform, start by accepting the email invitation sent to you.

Once you accept the invite, you will be able to access your dashboard and account. Follow the provided link to complete the account creation process. This step is crucial for getting your team set up and ready to use Chatform for seamless communication and collaboration.
